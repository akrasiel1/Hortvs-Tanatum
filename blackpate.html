Melchiorri Tartufata Black Truffle sauce (180 g., 6.35 oz)

Melchiorri’s Tartufata is a 100% natural sauce that combines fresh countryside mushrooms, Black Summer Truffles from Norcia and Spoleto, garlic, parsley and spices to create a unique experience for unadulterated food lovers. This magic condiment is amazingly versatile, turning any dish into something memorable. With absolutely no animals products, no artificial ingredients, no added sugars, no colouring or preservatives, no cholesterol, GLUTEN AND FAT FREE. Suitable for Coeliacs, Diabetics, Vegans and Vegetarians.

Allergen warning: Melchiorri’s Tartufata is produced in a facility that also handles celery, milk and nuts.
