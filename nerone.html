Nerone Acetaia San Donnino Balsamic Vinegar 6 years old (200 ml)

Nerone is a superior dressing vinegar aged over 6 years obtained from the cooked Lambrusco grapes that are allowed to mature by natural fermentation. Davide Lonardi produces his unique vinegar without using any additives such as flavour enhancers, colourings or sugar. Nerone is delicate, less dense but more versatile than other aged vinegars. Ideal with sushi and fillet steaks.
