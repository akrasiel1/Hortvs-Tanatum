Melchiorri’s “Intenditore” Single Estate Extra Virgin Olive Oil DOP Umbria (250 ml, 8.45 oz)

“Intenditore” is Melchiorri’s finest oil crafted exclusively from the best Assisi- Spoleto’s Moraiolo olives, carefully handpicked and first cold pressed at the end of October. Pure, unfiltered and produced in very limited quantities. This elegant Umbria DOP (Protected Designation of Origin) is buttery smooth, with intense yet harmonious notes of freshly cut grass, artichokes and black pepper. Delicious on seafood, vegetables, soups, stews or red meat

2017 Silver Medal NYIOOC/ New York’s World Olive Oil Competition
