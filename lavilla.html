Melchiorri’s “La Villa” Single Estate Extra Virgin Olive oil, 250 ml (8.45 oz)

This superior quality Extra Virgin Olive Oil is gently crafted from carefully selected Frantoio, Moraiolo and Leccino olives grown on Assisi and Spoleto hills, strictly harvested by hand, at the end of October. First cold pressed within few hours and unfiltered to create this exceptional “Finishing Oil”, to be enjoyed on salads, legumes, grilled meats and vegetables. Delicate and pleasantly fruity, with a green almond finish.
