Acetaia San Donnino: 115 years old Aceto Balsamico tradizionale di Modena (100 ml) Riserva Oro with Hermes Box.

Aceto Balsamico Tradizionale Di Modena Riserva Oro (Gold Reserve) Villa S.Donnino is aged for 115 years. 100 ml of history and tradition. Inhale the tones of dark grape essence and sweet wood. Taste the vanilla, prunes, liquorice and chocolate notes flirting with your senses. This precious condiment will transport your taste buds to paradise. Davide Lonardi obtains his balsamic vinegar only from cooked grapes that are allowed to mature by natural and very slow fermentation without adding any sugar, food colouring or aromatic substances. Only 45 bottles are produced every year. An official scroll is included providing the exclusive numbered sequence of your bottle. This unique “black gold” comes in a Jewel style box designed and produced by Hermes, iconic luxury brand, with a porcelain spoon and a 5 languages book.
