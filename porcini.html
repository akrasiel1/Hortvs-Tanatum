Melchiorri Porcini mushrooms Pâté (180 g., 6.35 oz)

A super natural blend of porcini mushrooms from Norcia and Spoleto, walnuts, countryside mushrooms, Marsala wine, onion and natural spices. The result of this delicate union is this gourmet sauce ideal for pasta, risotto, sauces, meats or crusty bread. Made with 100% natural ingredients in Melchiorri’s Single Estate. With absolutely no colouring and preservatives, no animal products, no GMO or added sugar. Suitable for Coeliacs, Vegans and vegetarians.

Allergen warning: Melchiorri’s Porcini Mushroom pâté is produced in a facility that also handles celery, milk and nuts.
